1. Who We Are / Quiénes somos

MedMio is an independent medication reminder application, not affiliated with any hospital, clinic, pharmacy, insurance company, or healthcare provider.

Contact: medmioapp@gmail.com

2. Scope / Alcance

This policy applies to the MedMio mobile app on Google Play and the Apple App Store. It does not apply to third-party websites or services linked from within the app.

3. Data We Collect / Datos que recopilamos

✅ MedMio does NOT collect, transmit, sell, share, or monetize any personal data by default.
✅ MedMio NO recopila, transmite, vende, comparte ni monetiza ningún dato personal por defecto.

MedMio does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any third-party tracking tools that transmit data off your device.

4. What Stays on Your Device

All data is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device and never transmitted:

  • Medication names and doses
  • Reminder schedules and times
  • Dose history and adherence records
  • Your display name (optional)
  • Language and display preferences
  • Medication photos (if added)
  • Food instructions and notes
  • Inventory counts

5. Optional OCR Feature

⚠️ This is optional — only activated if you tap the camera button. If you don't use it, no image data ever leaves your device.

When you photograph a medication label, the image may be sent to a secure server function to extract text via OCR. Images are processed in real time and immediately discarded — never stored on any server. The server does not log your identity, device ID, or medication name.

6. No Tracking, No Ads

✅ Zero advertising in MedMio.
✅ No tracking pixels, analytics libraries, advertising SDKs, or session replay tools.
✅ No data shared with advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing companies.
✅ No behavioral profiles built on users.

MedMio does NOT use: Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Sentry, Crashlytics, AppsFlyer, Adjust, Branch, or any analytics/advertising service.

7. No Account Required

No email, phone number, or any personal identifier is required. The name you optionally enter is stored only on your device.

8. Notifications

Reminders are scheduled entirely on your device using the times you configure. Notification content is processed locally and never transmitted to any server.

9. PDF Export

PDF reports are created entirely on your device. You control whether and with whom to share them. MedMio does not transmit PDFs to any server.

10. Data Retention

On your device: Data remains until you delete it, uninstall the app, or use "Delete All Data" in Settings. MedMio automatically removes dose history older than 18 months to maintain performance.

On our servers: MedMio stores no personal data on servers. OCR images are discarded immediately after processing.

11. How to Delete Your Data

  • In-app: Settings → Delete All Data → Confirm
  • Uninstall: Removes all locally stored data from your device

12. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (optional, OCR only): Processes medication label images when you use the optional OCR feature. No personal data is stored. supabase.com/privacy
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: Their policies govern download and installation.
  • Device OS notifications: Google and Apple policies apply to their notification infrastructure.

13. Security

  • Local database uses SQLite with WAL mode for data integrity
  • Sensitive identifiers stored in iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • All network communications use HTTPS
  • No passwords or financial data stored or processed

14. Children

MedMio is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact medmioapp@gmail.com if you believe a child has used the app.

15. HIPAA Notice

MedMio is not a HIPAA-covered entity and does not claim HIPAA compliance. All data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to or maintained by MedMio on any server.

⚠️ If you share MedMio data with a healthcare provider, that provider's own HIPAA obligations govern how they handle that information.

16. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Correction: Edit any medication, schedule, or profile data in the app
  • Deletion: See Section 11
  • Portability: Use the PDF export feature
  • Opt-out of OCR: Simply don't use the camera feature

California residents: MedMio does not meet CCPA thresholds but voluntarily provides the rights above to all users. EU/EEA residents: No personal data is transferred to servers in any jurisdiction.

17. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. Previous versions available by emailing medmioapp@gmail.com.

18. Contact

Email: medmioapp@gmail.com
We respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.
